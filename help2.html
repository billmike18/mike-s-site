Delivery Time

We're excited to offer delivery services for your favorite baked goods. Orders are typically delivered within **4 to 5 business days**.

At our bakery, we don't just create delicious cakes—we're also happy to style cakes and other pastries that you bring to us. Whether it’s adding a custom design, decorating for a special event, or giving your treats a professional finish, we’re here to help!
